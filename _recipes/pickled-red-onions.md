# Pickled Red Onions

## Ingredients
- Red onion, sliced
- 2 cups vinegar (apple cider or red wine)
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp white granulated sugar

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.
2. Simmer until onions soften.
3. Eat.
