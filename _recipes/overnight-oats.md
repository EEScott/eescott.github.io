# Overnight Oats

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/4 cup yogurt
- 1 tsp chia seeds
- 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
- Pinch of salt

**Toppings:** fruit, nuts, nut butter, granola, raisins
